Dr. Andrews releases 'Throw Like A Pro'
app to help reduce elbow injuries

Dr. James Andrews and Dr. Kevin Wilk have released the
"Throw Like A Pro" app, designed to help reduce the number
of elbow injuries, specifically Tommy John surgery. They
"anticipate a 60% reduction in overuse related baseball
injuries in the coming years" if the app is used properly,
according to the press release.
